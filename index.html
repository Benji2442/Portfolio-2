About me

I am a budding, self taught web designer in Leeds who has a real passion for creating fully responsive websites that look great on any screen size. I have been using the treehouse website to gain the knowledge I needed to start creating using technologies such as html, css, javascript, jquery, sass, bootstrap and git. Once I gained enough knowledge I started putting it to use creating websites for people I knew and ultimately honing my skills with the goal of landing a web designer job. I have always been quite a creative individual going from drawing, to painting, to two big passions in my life, photography and guitar playing, all of which I taught myself how to do. If I want to learn something I have always had the drive to go out and get the answers and knowledge I need.
